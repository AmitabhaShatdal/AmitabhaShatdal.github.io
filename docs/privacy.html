Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Alpha Quelle does not collect, store, or process any personal data from our users. We do not use cookies or any other tracking technologies to monitor your activity on our site. Your privacy is important to us, and we believe in providing our service without compromising your personal information.

Advertising

Alpha Quelle displays advertisements from select partners. These advertisements are served directly by Alpha Quelle and are not personalized based on your browsing history or personal data.

Our Advertising Approach

  • We do not use third-party ad networks or programmatic advertising.
  • Advertisements displayed on our site are curated partnerships with select brands.
  • No tracking cookies or behavioral data are used to serve advertisements.
  • Your browsing activity on Alpha Quelle is not shared with advertisers.

Data We Do Not Collect

Changes to This Policy
